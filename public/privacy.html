Privacy Policy

ArtVision Pro — Last updated: April 17, 2026

This policy describes how ArtVision Pro (“the app”) handles information when you use our website or installed progressive web app. If you use a deployment operated by someone else, that operator is responsible for their hosting configuration; this document describes typical behavior of the open-source product.

Contact. For privacy questions, contact the publisher of the app version you installed (for example the email or support link shown on the app store listing or project page). Replace this sentence on your fork with your legal name and contact email if you self-host.

What we collect and why

Painting images you submit for critique or AI preview. When you run a critique or generate an AI preview, your image is sent from your device to the app’s API server so it can be analyzed or edited. That server may call third-party AI providers (for example OpenAI) to produce results. Images are processed for your request and are not used by this software to train public models; however, third-party AI providers apply their own terms and retention policies, which you should review on their sites.

Optional payments (Stripe). If the operator enables billing, payments for critiques or AI previews are processed by Stripe. We do not receive your full card number on our servers; Stripe handles payment data according to its privacy policy.

Product analytics. Deployments may include Vercel Analytics or similar tools to measure traffic and performance. Those services may collect limited technical data (for example page views, device type, and region) as described in their policies.

Data stored on your device

The app can save paintings, critique history, and preferences locally in your browser (for example using local storage or similar). That data stays on your device unless you export it or the app explicitly syncs to a backend you configure. Clearing site data or uninstalling the app may delete this local information.

Who we share data with

  • AI providers (when you use critique or AI preview features) — to generate results.
  • Stripe (when paid features are enabled) — to authorize and capture payments.
  • Hosting / analytics vendors (for example Vercel) — to operate the service and understand reliability and usage.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Operators should not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent under local law.

Your choices

  • You can avoid uploading images by not using critique or AI preview features.
  • You can clear browser storage to remove locally saved works and settings.
  • Where payments are optional, you can decline paid actions.

Changes

We may update this policy when features or legal requirements change. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy unless applicable law requires otherwise.
